Matching Bears

How to Play

There are eight sets of matching bears. Can you find them all before the timer runs out? Select your difficulty from the drop down box and click a square to begin.

Easy - you have 100 seconds to find the matches
Medium - you have 60 seconds to find the matches
Hard - you have 30 seconds to find the matches
